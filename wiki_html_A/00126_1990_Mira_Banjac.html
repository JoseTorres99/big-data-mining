Mira Banjac (Serbian Cyrillic: Мира Бањац; born 4 November 1929) is a Serbian actress.

She won a number of awards, including an award for her work in Mamaroš from Brazil[1] and Golden Medal for Merits of Republic of Serbia.[2]

Personal life

[edit]

Banjac was married to architect Andrej Jovanović. In 1951 they had a son Branislav. Branislav died on February 6, 2025 at the age of 74 from a short illness.
