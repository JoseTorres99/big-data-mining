Lewis Van Bergen (November 9, 1938 – April 6, 2018)[1][2] was an American actor, best known for his role as Jon Sable on the short-lived 1987 television series Sable.

He was in the film Bugsy in the role of Joey Adonis, with whom Bugsy Siegel competed for the affection of Virginia Hill.

Death

[edit]

Van Bergen died on April 6, 2018, at the age of 79. His cause of death is unknown.[2]
