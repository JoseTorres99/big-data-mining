Mason Gamble (born January 16, 1986) is an American former actor who played the eponymous comic character in the 1993 film Dennis the Menace, selected from over 20,000 children.[2] He also appeared as Max Fischer's young accomplice, Dirk Calloway, in Wes Anderson's film Rushmore.

Education

[edit]

As of 2016[update], Gamble has a doctoral candidate in environmental science and engineering at the Institute of the Environment and Sustainability at UCLA.[3]
