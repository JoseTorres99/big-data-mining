Dilip Roy (Bengali: দিলীপ রায়) (17 December 1931 – 2 September 2010) was an Indian Bengali film actor and director.[3] He worked in Bengali films. Roy was born in Chittagong, Bangladesh.

Death

[edit]

Roy died on 2 September 2010 at Kolkata, India at the age of 79 due to cancer.[4]
