Dutch film director (born 1953)

In this Dutch name, the surname is de Jong, not Jong.

Years active1976–present

Ate de Jong (born 1953) is a Dutch film and television director, film producer, and screenwriter. He is best known as the producer of The Discovery of Heaven (2001), nominated for a Golden Calf award, and Het Bombardement (2012).[1]
