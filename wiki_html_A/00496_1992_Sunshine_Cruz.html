Sunshine Braden Cruz (Tagalog pronunciation: [ˈkɾus]; born July 18, 1977)[1] is a Filipino actress and singer. She is part of the Cruz family of actors.

Personal life

[edit]

Cruz married actor and director Cesar Montano on September 14, 2000. They have three daughters: Angelina Isabelle, Samantha Angelene "Sam", and Angel Franchesca "Chesca". In 2013, the couple separated and in 2018, the marriage was annulled by the Quezon City Court.[2][3]

In 2016, she dated Ara Mina's half-brother Macky Mathay, but she admitted their breakup in 2022.[4]

In 2024, businessman Atong Ang confirmed that he was in a relationship with Cruz.[5]

In 2025, Cruz disclosed that she had been diagnosed with myasthenia gravis.[6]
